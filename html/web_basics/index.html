I love Summertime!

I just thought I would take the time to let you know how much better summer is than winter!

Reasons why summer is the best season

  • you can go outside to play sports, take a walk, or just enjoy the wildflowers
  • no one gets sick as often in summer
  • the days are longer, which means you can still enjoy some daylight after you get home from work (and witness a beautiful sunset)
